flat.css is an experimental css framework that explores a truly "flat" / non-skeomorphic design.

My own view is that skeumorphism is very useful and that the flattening of design will be a gradual process--the need to anchor UI elements in the physical world will diminish as web design become a universal aesthetic in its own right. In the meantime, flat.css skips ahead to the logical conclusion.

But the result was cool enough that I used it for my home page. It's written in SASS, stands on top of normalize.css and lives here
